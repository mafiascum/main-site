How to Join your first game!

Welcome to MafiaScum! Below you’ll find the quick and dirty on how to get started on mafiascum.net.

How to get started:

First you’ll want to register an account. Do so by clicking the “Register” link in the top right hand corner of the page. Fill out and submit the form, then go check your email. There should be an email with a link to activate your account (don’t forget to check your spam folder if it’s not there). Click that and you’re good to go!  

Joining a game:

-The Queue

Now that you’re registered, you can enter a queue to play in a game. Click the “Queue” forum, and search for the kind of game you’d like to join. We recommend new users start with our Newbie Queue to help get you acclimated to the site and the game itself. Here you’ll find games that use a set format with experienced players and moderators to help ease your transition here. (It’s important to note that because Newbie games have certain requirements to start, it sometimes takes a little while to have enough players to start a game.)

The other games in the Queue include:

Open Games: Games of various sizes which use a known setup
Micro Games: Games with fewer than 9 players and can be themed or normal
Mini (10-13 players) and Large (14+ players) Normal Games: Games using only basic roles and standard mechanics
Mini (10-13 players) and Large Theme Games (14+ players): Games that are based on source material and/or include changes to mechanics or rules.

For more information about our Queue, check out the wiki page (link wiki page).

-Signing Up

To join a game, simply go to the queue of the type of game you’re interested in, and reply with “/in” to join. Some queues require that you specify which game you want to play, as they have multiple games in sign ups at once. Be sure to read the first post of the queue before positng to get that queue’s specific rules.

If you’re looking to get into a game immediately, you may be interested in replacing into a game that has already started. To replace into a Newbie game, check the Newbie Queue for mods looking for replacements. For all other games, check the “Replacement Requests” thread in the Queue forum.

-Now What?

Now that you’ve signed up for a game, all you have to do is wait for a Private Message from the game moderator. This should include your role, any instructions to confirm your role, and will typically include links to any threads you may need.  This may take a while, so feel to browse the rest of the site while you wait!

Discussion Forums:

Mafiascum.net has a lively community that enjoys talking about a variety of subjects. In the Mafia Discussion forum, you can talk about all things game related (as long as you don’t talk about a game that is currently ongoing). The General Discussion Forum is where you’ll find topics that aren’t Mafia related. There is usually something in there for everyone, so check it out!

There are two subforums under General Discussion for which you have to opt-in (and meet minimum requirements for). The Speakeasy is home to the more mature conversations that aren’t really appropriate for GD. Get to Know a Scummer (or GTKAS) is where you’ll learn about the other members onsite and can /in to have your own thread  if you want us get to know you!

MishMash and LSG:

When you're between games, indulge yourself at our four-star MafiaScum Buffet! MafiaScum also hosts a variety of other games in our MishMash and Large Social game forums. In MishMash you’ll find everything from board games to game shows to original games. There’s something for everyone! The Large Social Games Queue is home to our forum versions of Survivor, Big Brother, and other larger games (over 12 players) that focus primarily on social interactions.

Administration:

If you’re having issues with your account or have found a bug on the site, etc., you’ll want to visit the Help forum. Here you can post with a guest account if you’re having an issue getting your account set up.

We’re always striving to make the site a better place, so if you have any ideas, feel free to post them in Site Ideas! You can also weigh in on other people’s posts or visit the Player Titles subforum to learn about one of MafiaScum’s oldest traditions of gifting titles to players.

Lastly, you can find the latest information on what’s happening around the site in the News section.  This includes the state of the site, financial summaries, new features, and anything else relating to MS.

Site Chat:

Site Chat is a real time chat room where you can chat with other Scummers in the Lobby, a private room, or one on one in a private conversation. It is automatically turned on, and you’ll find it in the bottom right hand corner of your screen. To disable this feature, click “Turn off chat” at the top left of the page next to the User Control Panel. You’ll also find various settings in the “Site Chat” tab.
